Lok Sewa


It was established on 15 June 1951 (वि.सं. २००८ साल असार १). Loksewa Aayog, Public Service Commission PSC is mentioned under the Constitution of Nepal under Part 23.Loksewa is a competitive exam to get various governmental/civil service jobs and increase their ranks in Nepal.

E-notes


eNotes is a free student and teacher educational website that provides material to help students complete homework assignments and study for exams. E-Notes specializes in lesson plans, study guides and literary criticisms. It also hosts an active homework help portal where students can ask educators academic questions.

Book Club


A book club is a reading group, usually consisting of a number of people who read and talk about books based on a topic or an agreed-upon reading list. It's common for book clubs to choose a specific book to read and discuss at the same time. Formal book clubs meet on a regular basis at a set location.
